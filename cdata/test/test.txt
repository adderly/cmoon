实例: http://rtds.dsc.hunantv.com/cgi-bin/cds.cgi?key=6&op=n_video&query=get



三个参数的取值范围:

key: 1 ~ 10000000
op : n_user_space, n_photo, n_photo_album, n_video, n_video_d, n_video_album, n_blog
query: get, put


请将这三个参数组合测试, 如果时间太长的话可以把 key 的取值范围缩小

组1(换 key):

key=1&op=n_user_space&query=get
key=2&op=n_user_space&query=get
key=3&op=n_user_space&query=get
...
key=10000000&op=n_user_space&query=get




组2(换 op):
key=1&op=n_photo&query=get
key=2&op=n_photo&query=get
key=3&op=n_photo&query=get
...
key=10000000&op=n_photo&query=get




组3(换 query)
key=1&op=n_user_space&query=put
key=2&op=n_user_space&query=put
key=3&op=n_user_space&query=put
...
key=10000000&op=n_user_space&query=put
